Coaching for Clarity,
Growth & Meaningful Connection

Communication shapes every part of life - at work, at home, and within ourselves. When misunderstandings or conflict arise, it can be difficult to express yourself in a way that truly connects.

I offer personal coaching to help you communicate with while navigating life's challenges. Whether you're facing personal struggles, relationship tensions, or professional transitions, my approach provides a supportive space for self-awareness, deeper connection, and meaningful change. You'll tap into your inner wisdom and develop communication skills as you move forward with confidence and purpose.

Based in Haarlem, I coach in English and Dutch, drawing on over 30 years of experience in communication, leadership, and conflict resolution.

Coaching for Growth & Connection

I work primarily with expats living in the Randstad area, the Netherlands and also online with people around the world who want to experience being more heard, understood, and confident in their personal and professional lives.

Next to coaching individuals, I also help people wanting to work through differences and resolve conflict in connecting ways. This might be between 2 individuals, in a family context or within an existing group of people living or working together.

I also offer coaching for groups & teams wanting to learn how to bridge differences, and build skills for inclusive leadership, collaborative decision-making and connecting communication skills..

Cara Crisler

I'm passionate about helping others to find their way.

I love supporting human beings, wishing each of us to experience joy and meaning in our daily lives. Not at the expense of others, but in cooperation and through mutual support.

For the past thirty years, my career has been centered on supporting people. As a senior-level EMCC-accredited coach, I help people navigate challenges, build confidence, and develop meaningful connections. My experience as a certified Nonviolent Communication trainer allows me to create a safe, nonjudgmental space for people to explore their needs, express themselves clearly, listen empathically and thereby engage in transformative conversations.

I am dedicated to offering support in a sustained way. I value authenticity, connection, equity and true cooperation.
