Personal Coaching

A confidential space to explore challenges, break patterns, and gain clarity. Individual coaching sessions are fully personalized to your needs - whether you're struggling with communication, personal growth, or life transitions. Our conversations focus on self-awareness, emotional intelligence, and practical tools to help you express yourself with confidence and ease.

Common areas I help with:

  • Breaking old patterns that no longer serve you.
  • Finding meaningful connection in your personal and professional life.
  • Developing skills to handle confrontational or conflict situations.
  • Learning how to manage your inner critic.
  • Setting boundaries and saying no with confidence.
  • Navigating grief, loss, or major life changes.
  • Balancing work and personal life.
  • Understanding and managing anger - your own or others'.
  • Adjusting to the Dutch Culture.
